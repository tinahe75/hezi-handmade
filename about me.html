ABOUT ME
I'm Tina (aka Hezi). Aside from eating a ridiculous amount of bread, I also like to make stuff. I hand-paint jewelry (mostly pendants), and experiment with polymer clay, needle felt and textiles.

This website is a collection of some of the things I've made since 2013. Please enjoy:)
